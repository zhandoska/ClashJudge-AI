Our Story

Why We Exist: We are student debaters who built the tool we wished we had.

The Problem: Most feedback tools focus on style. We focus on structure, logic, and argument strength.

The Story: ClashJudge IO started as a school project to bring fair, structured, AI-based feedback to the debate world. Now, it’s growing into a platform that helps students across the globe.

How It Works

1. Upload

Submit your debate video/audio or a written argument draft.

2. AI Analyzes

We use advanced models to check your logic, structure, and strength of points.

3. Get Feedback

Instant, constructive, rubric-based suggestions—no style judgments.

Get Feedback

Paste or upload your written argument. Great for prep or training.

About Us

ClashJudge IO was founded by high school debaters passionate about fair feedback. We’ve led Olympiads, TEDx events, and debate clubs—and we wanted to give back. This platform is student-made, student-led, and student-focused.

Support Us

Right now, ClashJudge IO is free for all. In the future, we’ll introduce Pro features and one-on-one feedback sessions. If you believe in our mission, you can support us through donations or by joining the team.
