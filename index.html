About ClashJudge

ClashJudge IO is a student-driven platform designed to help debaters improve through objective feedback. Our AI evaluates your arguments' structure, content, and strength—ignoring delivery style to focus purely on substance.

Features

Content-Based Feedback

Receive critiques focused solely on the strength and relevance of your arguments—not your confidence, voice, or gestures.

Written & Video Input

Upload full debate videos or written cases to receive comprehensive, AI-generated feedback instantly.

Student-Led Story

Born out of real student need, ClashJudge IO reflects the values of empowerment, fairness, and growth.
